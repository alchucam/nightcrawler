Welcome to NightCrawler!

This is a simple experimental website to display most popular international news along with their links.
Are you a busy person? Are you tired of reading all the news just to catch up with all the topics people are talking about?
You've come to the right place! You can find out all the most mentioned keywords in the global news!
And you can check the sentiment scores to see whether the news articles are being positive or negative!

Here's some information for you.
News Tab: The website parses and analyzes the context to determine the sentiment scores and most frequent words of the contexts.
nytimes: New York Times (USA). yonhap: Yonhap News Agency (South Korea).
ecns: English-language website of China News Service (China). japantimes: The Japan Times (Japan).

Analaysis Tab: The international current state of affairs are analyzed using the average sentiment scores from the number of articles mentioned the selected country.
How to use analysis tab: First, choose a country to select the source country of news articles.
Then, choose any countries below to select the target countries that were mentioned in the source country of news articles.
You will be able to view the average sentiment scores of the news articles of source country mentioning the target countries.
You will also be able to view the ratio of news articles mentioning the target countries to the total news articles of source country.

Sentiment Analysis: the scores are determined using NLTK package.
Score 0 represents neutral, 1 represents the most positive and -1 represents the most negative sentiment.

The timezone is based on UTC +0.
Please enjoy!

Languages used:

Front End: HTML, Javascript, CSS
Back End: Python
Database: MySQL
Libraries: BeautifulSoup, NLTK, HighCharts
